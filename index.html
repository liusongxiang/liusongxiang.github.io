Short Bio

My name is Songxiang Liu (刘颂湘), currently a 3rd-year PhD student in the Department of System Engineering and Engineering Management at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Helen Meng. My research interests are Voice Conversion, Speech Synthesis and Deep Learning.

Before that, I obtained B.S. degree in Automation from the Department of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University.
